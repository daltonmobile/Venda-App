Privacy Policy

Learn Tshivenda with Mutodi

Last updated: May 2026
🔒 No Data Collected 👤 No Account Required 🙊 Child Safe 🌐 Works Offline

🔒 Our Simple Promise

Learn Tshivenda with Mutodi does not collect, store, share or sell any personal information from its users — including children. Ever.

This app was created as an educational tool to help children learn the Tshivenda language. It is designed to be completely private and safe for all ages, including young children.

👤 Who Uses This App

This app is designed for children of all ages and their families. It is a language learning app covering Grades 1 through 6 of the Tshivenda language curriculum.

The app is intended for use within a family or educational setting and is not a commercial platform.

📋 What Information We Collect

We collect absolutely no personal information. None.

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • Names, email addresses or any contact information
  • Location data of any kind
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Photos, microphone or camera data
  • Payment or financial information
  • Any data from children under the age of 13

🌍 Internet & Audio

The app uses an internet connection solely to stream Tshivenda audio pronunciation files hosted on GitHub. These are pre-recorded educational audio clips.

No user data is transmitted when audio is played. The connection is one-way (download only) and entirely anonymous.

The app does not access your microphone, camera, contacts, location or any other device feature beyond playing audio.

👤 Children's Privacy (COPPA)

We take children's privacy seriously and fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and equivalent South African and international regulations.

  • We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13
  • No registration or account creation is required to use the app
  • No in-app purchases, ads or third-party tracking
  • No chat, messaging or social features
  • No links to external websites within the app
  • No behavioural profiling of any user

🏭 Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services in a limited and privacy-safe manner:

  • GitHub (audio hosting) — Audio files are streamed from a public GitHub repository. GitHub may log anonymous access requests as part of standard server operation. No user data is sent to GitHub.
  • Google Fonts — The app loads the Quicksand font from Google Fonts when online. This is a standard web font service. No personal data is shared.
  • Unsplash (images) — Category images are loaded from Unsplash's public image CDN. No personal data is shared.

None of these services receive any personal information about users of this app.

🔐 Data Storage

The app stores no data on any server. The only local storage used is the device's standard audio and image cache, which is managed by Android and contains no personal information.

Any profile name selected within the app (Alexis, Sadee or Leslee) is stored only in the device's browser/app memory for the duration of the session and is never transmitted anywhere.

📝 Advertising

This app contains no advertisements of any kind. It is completely ad-free.

We do not use any advertising networks, tracking pixels, or marketing technologies. There are no in-app purchases.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the updated version will be published at this same URL with a new "Last updated" date. We will never introduce data collection without updating this policy and notifying users.

Given that the app collects no data, changes to this policy are unlikely.
